About SafeZone

SafeZone serves as a centralized platform that integrates the university's medical, security, and fire safety services. In the event of an incident, users can report the issue through the SafeZone app, which then alerts the appropriate responders—medical, security, or fire services—based on the nature of the incident. This integration ensures a coordinated and efficient response, leveraging the existing infrastructure and expertise of the university's departments. By connecting users directly with the university's established services, SafeZone enhances the overall safety and well-being of the campus community, providing a seamless and effective response to emergencies.

How to Use SafeZone

Step 1: Sign Up

Register on the platform by providing your details to access all features.

Step 2: Report an Incident

Once registered, you can report any emergency or safety incident by filling out a form with necessary details.

Step 3: Receive Assistance

SafeZone will notify the nearest responders and emergency contacts, ensuring swift intervention in your location.
